ANDRÉ PARR

BOHRIALI

Web Design & Dev., Ultimate Mango

A variety of clients with a greater variety of needs came up to us to bring their websites to life. One such example is of Bohriali. They've been in the business for 140+ years and they're going further. The project was to create the perfect landing page that effeciently links the various section of it to a number of places, like the Bohriali blog, or the e-commerce platform that was newly started by them. We also created a small calculator that could approximately quote your painting cost depending on your paint preferences.

From the website, to social media creatives, to designing a mascot — Bohriali Baba, it was great fun working with them and seeing a century old business model take a digital leap of faith.
